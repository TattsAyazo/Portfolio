the wonder

Concepting, brand strategy and end-to-end ideation of an email newsletter brand containing links to fascinating content from around the web. Each edition is curated and written by members of Likemind’s in-house editorial team Keeping in mind the mother brand tagline "Expanding perspectives" Wanted to capture and provoke that sense of wonder. There was an influence of sacred geometry meeting Bruno Munari ideals of geometrical shapes. Square, Circle and triangle make part of our everyday lives and exist in those magical tiny corners of the world where we can't even see them. What the outcome was an engaging emotive brand that provokes users to be more curious and explore knowledge in a lighthearted and inclusive way.
